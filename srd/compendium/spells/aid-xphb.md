# Aid
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 30 feet
- **Components:** V, S, M (a strip of white cloth)
- **Duration:** 8 hours

Choose up to three creatures within range. Each target's [Hit Point](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) maximum and current [Hit Points](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) increase by 5 for the duration.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** Each target's [Hit Points](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) increase by 5 for each spell slot level above 2.

**Classes**: [Bard (College of Lore)](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-college-of-lore-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Bard](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric (Life Domain)](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-life-domain-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Cleric](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md); [Paladin (Oath of Devotion)](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-oath-of-devotion-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Paladin](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md); [Ranger](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-ranger.md); [Sorcerer (Clockwork Sorcery)](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-clockwork-sorcery-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB"); [Warlock (Celestial Patron)](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-celestial-patron-xphb.md "subclass=XPHB;class=XPHB")
