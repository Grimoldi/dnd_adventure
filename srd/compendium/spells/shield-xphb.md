# Shield
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*1st-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Reaction
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** 1 round

An imperceptible barrier of magical force protects you. Until the start of your next turn, you have a +5 bonus to AC, including against the triggering attack, and you take no damage from [Magic Missile](srd/compendium/spells/magic-missile-xphb.md).
