# Invisibility
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*2nd-level, Illusion*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (an eyelash in gum arabic)
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 1 hour

A creature you touch has the [Invisible](srd/rules/conditions.md#Invisible) condition until the spell ends. The spell ends early immediately after the target makes an attack roll, deals damage, or casts a spell.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** You can target one additional creature for each spell slot level above 2.
