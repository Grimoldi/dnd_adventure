# Aura of Purity
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*4th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Self (30-foot Emanation)
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** Concentration, up to 10 minutes

An aura radiates from you in a 30-foot [Emanation](srd/rules/variant-rules/emanation-area-of-effect-xphb.md) for the duration. While in the aura, you and your allies have [Resistance](srd/rules/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to Poison damage and [Advantage](srd/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on saving throws to avoid or end effects that include the [Blinded](srd/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [Charmed](srd/rules/conditions.md#Charmed), [Deafened](srd/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), [Frightened](srd/rules/conditions.md#Frightened), [Paralyzed](srd/rules/conditions.md#Paralyzed), [Poisoned](srd/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned), or [Stunned](srd/rules/conditions.md#Stunned) condition.

**Classes**: [Bard](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Paladin](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-paladin.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 244*
