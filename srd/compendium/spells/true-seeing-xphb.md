# True Seeing
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*6th-level, Divination*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** Touch
- **Components:** V, S, M (mushroom powder worth 25+ GP, which the spell consumes)
- **Duration:** 1 hour

For the duration, the willing creature you touch has [Truesight](srd/rules/senses.md#Truesight) with a range of 120 feet.
