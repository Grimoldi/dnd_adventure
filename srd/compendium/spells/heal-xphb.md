# Heal
%%-- Embedded content starts on the next line. --%%
*6th-level, Abjuration*  

- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 60 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

Choose a creature that you can see within range. Positive energy washes through the target, restoring 70 [Hit Points](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md). This spell also ends the [Blinded](srd/rules/conditions.md#Blinded), [Deafened](srd/rules/conditions.md#Deafened), and [Poisoned](srd/rules/conditions.md#Poisoned) conditions on the target.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** The healing increases by `10` for each spell slot level above 6.

**Classes**: [Bard](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-bard.md); [Cleric](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-cleric.md); [Druid](srd/compendium/lists/list-spells-classes-druid.md)

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 284. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
