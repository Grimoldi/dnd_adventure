# Scholar's Pack
*Adventuring gear*  

- **Cost**: 40 gp
- **Weight**: 22.0 lbs.

A Scholar's Pack contains the following items: [Backpack](srd/compendium/items/backpack-xphb.md), [Book](srd/compendium/items/book-xphb.md), [Ink](srd/compendium/items/ink-xphb.md), [Ink Pen](srd/compendium/items/ink-pen-xphb.md), [Lamp](srd/compendium/items/lamp-xphb.md), 10 flasks of [Oil](srd/compendium/items/oil-xphb.md), 10 sheets of [Parchment](srd/compendium/items/parchment-xphb.md), and [Tinderbox](srd/compendium/items/tinderbox-xphb.md).

*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 228. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
