# Durable
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 203*  

**Prerequisite**: 4th

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase your Constitution score by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Defy Death.** You have [Advantage](srd/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md) on [Death Saving Throws](srd/rules/variant-rules/death-saving-throw-xphb.md).

**Speedy Recovery.** As a [Bonus Action](srd/rules/variant-rules/bonus-action-xphb.md), you can expend one of your [Hit Point Dice](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-point-dice-xphb.md), roll the die, and regain a number of [Hit Points](srd/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) equal to the roll.
