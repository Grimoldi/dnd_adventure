# Actions
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 15. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)* 

**Actions**

| Action | Summary |
|--------|---------|
| [Attack](srd/rules/actions.md#Attack) | Attack with a weapon or an [Unarmed Strike](srd/rules/variant-rules/unarmed-strike-xphb.md). |
| [Dash](srd/rules/actions.md#Dash) | For the rest of the turn, give yourself extra movement equal to your [Speed](srd/rules/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md). |
| [Disengage](srd/rules/actions.md#Disengage) | Your movement doesn't provoke [Opportunity Attack](srd/rules/actions.md#Opportunity%20Attack) for the rest of the turn. |
| [Dodge](srd/rules/actions.md#Dodge) | Until the start of your next turn, attack rolls against you have [Disadvantage](srd/rules/variant-rules/disadvantage-xphb.md), and you make Dexterity saving throws with [Advantage](srd/rules/variant-rules/advantage-xphb.md). You lose this benefit if you have the [Incapacitated](srd/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition or if your Speed is 0. |
| [Help](srd/rules/actions.md#Help) | Help another creature's ability check or attack roll, or administer first aid. |
| [Hide](srd/rules/actions.md#Hide) | Make a Dexterity ([Stealth](srd/rules/skills.md#Stealth)) check. |
| [Influence](srd/rules/actions.md#Influence) | Make a Charisma ([Deception](srd/rules/skills.md#Deception), [Intimidation](srd/rules/skills.md#Intimidation), [Performance](srd/rules/skills.md#Performance), or [Persuasion](srd/rules/skills.md#Persuasion)) or Wisdom ([Animal Handling](srd/rules/skills.md#Animal%20Handling)) check to alter a creature's attitude. |
| [Magic](srd/rules/actions.md#Magic) | Cast a spell, use a magic item, or use a magical feature. |
| [Ready](srd/rules/actions.md#Ready) | Prepare to take an action in response to a trigger you define. |
| [Search](srd/rules/actions.md#Search) | Make a Wisdom ([Insight](srd/rules/skills.md#Insight), [Medicine](srd/rules/skills.md#Medicine), [Perception](srd/rules/skills.md#Perception), or [Survival](srd/rules/skills.md#Survival)) check. |
| [Study](srd/rules/actions.md#Study) | Make an Intelligence ([Arcana](srd/rules/skills.md#Arcana), [History](srd/rules/skills.md#History), [Investigation](srd/rules/skills.md#Investigation), [Nature](srd/rules/skills.md#Nature), or [Religion](srd/rules/skills.md#Religion)) check. |
| [Utilize](srd/rules/actions.md#Utilize) | Use a nonmagical object. |
^actions
